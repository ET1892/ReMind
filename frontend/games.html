Learn Games Facts
Minesweeper

The number on a block shows the number of mines adjacent to it and you have to flag all the mines
Scramble

Unscramble letters to form words before time runs out
Sudoku

Fill the remaining 9x9 grid with numbers 1 to 9, there is no repeated numbers allowed in a square, row, or column
Recall

Memorise and match hidden cards
Tetris

Rotate and stack falling blocks to clear lines. Keep the board from filling up
